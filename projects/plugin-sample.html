Introducing Pentaho Community Edition 5.0

Pentaho Community Edition is the place for the open source developers, promoting innovation, participation and cooperation. It also introduces Pentaho Marketplace, where community members can explore all the available plugins at a distance of a click.

An engagement mechanism
  • Participate in the Pentaho Community: By using Pentaho CE software you are also participating in the Pentaho Community and contributing to an open source project.
  • Make valuable contributions: The open source project delivers better, faster and reliable products, empowered by an active and wider community. Developers, testers, writers, implementers, and most of all users can make valuable contributions.
A QA environment
  • Test it: There are no better testers than people who actually use the Pentaho CE software, reporting bugs and making suggestions.
An evaluation territory
  • Build it as better as you can: Pentaho CE is an evaluation territory and creating a good expectation to the Pentaho Enterprise Edition is the key.
